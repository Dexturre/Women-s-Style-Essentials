Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

At Women's Style Essentials, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information from you in several ways, including:

  • Log Data: When you visit our site, our servers automatically record information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and track site usage. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience.
  • Communication: If you contact us directly via email or our contact form, we may receive your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • Improve and personalize your browsing experience.
  • Analyze how you use our website to help us create better content.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support.

3. Third-Party Links & Services

Our website contains links to third-party websites, including Amazon. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any website you visit after leaving ours.

4. Children's Privacy

Women's Style Essentials does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you. For any inquiries, please contact us through our dedicated contact page.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
